Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 3-5 business days. Domestic shipping takes 5-7 business days. We are not accepting international orders at this time.

What is your return policy?

If you are not happy with your purchase, please contact mini_kobo@outlook.com for support.

What if my package is lost or delayed?

You will receive a tracking number via email. If your order is delayed, please check your tracking information first, then contact your local post office for assistance.

Can I cancel my order?

Please contact mini_kobo@outlook.com for support. However please understand that once your order is shipped, cancellations won't be possible.
